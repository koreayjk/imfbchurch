About
Jikwon (Moses) Yu
Senior Pastor · IM Friend Baptist Church

Pastor Moses Yu's journey began with a radical encounter with grace at the age of 22. Accepting Jesus as his personal Savior changed everything, igniting a passion to bring hope to hopeless youth. He dedicated his life to the path of an Educational Missionary.

Today, Pastor Moses and his wife Juliet serve together in Bryan/College Station, raising the next generation of Kingdom leaders alongside their two daughters and one son.

M.Div. — Drew University, Madison, NJ
20+ years of campus ministry & discipleship
Ordained — Southern Baptist Convention (SBC), October 2024
